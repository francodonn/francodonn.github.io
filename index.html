About

Hello, I’m Franco Donnangelo. My main work is on the scaling of lab solutions (in ML and Quantum) so their impact can be maximized. My experience has led me to build and scale complex solutions in the Fintech and E-commerce industries.

I’m mainly focused on industrial applications and occasionally I work on academic projects, some examples include my Master’s and Engineering degree thesis (see below). I like to work in results-oriented environments, where both creativity and great ideas can be seen at their peak.
